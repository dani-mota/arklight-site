What Makes ACI Different

Built Different Because the Problem Is Different

Behavioral Integrity as a Core Dimension
The hardest thing to assess isn't whether they're smart enough. It's whether they'll follow the procedure when no one is watching. Whether they'll return the tools. Whether they'll flag an anomaly instead of overlooking it. Most assessment platforms don't touch this. ACI measures it as a first-class dimension.
Depth Over Speed
The market is racing toward shorter assessments. Five minutes. Three minutes. We went the other direction — because your roles deserve it. ACI is a thorough, structured investigation. The candidates right for your environment will invest in a serious evaluation. The ones who won't? That's signal, too.
Methodology, Not Just Technology
Most AI assessment tools are technology looking for a problem. ACI is methodology first, technology second. The architecture is rooted in decades of research into identifying talent for high-stakes roles. The AI serves the methodology — not the other way around.
Purpose-Built for Critical Environments
ACI wasn't adapted from a consumer hiring platform. It was built from the ground up for environments where assessment must be deeper, more rigorous, and designed for stakes that most tools were never intended to handle. Your environment cannot afford to guess.
