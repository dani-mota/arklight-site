Arklight Cognitive Index: Identifying high-potential Americans that traditional hiring overlooks.

Research Foundation

What ACI Measures
Cognitive Capability
Predicts and measures whether someone can solve and master complex work, regardless of background or credentials.
Behavioral & Integrity
Analyzes and predicts an individual's decision-making under pressure, when conditions are hard, stakes are real, and no one is watching.
Technical Aptitude
Quantifies a candidate's ability for technical tasks and workflows, to operate in high-precision environments.
Every candidate benchmarked against top operators in the U.S. industrial base. Resumes show where someone has been. ACI reveals what they're capable of becoming.
