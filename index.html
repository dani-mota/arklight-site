Building Predictable, High-Quality Talent Capacity for American Industrial Base

Project Arklight solves talent bottlenecks for advanced manufacturing and defense, helping partners scale production and unlock revenue faster.

Partner Problems We Solve

Talent Scarcity
With 4 million roles projected to go unfilled by 2030, traditional sourcing is mathematically incapable of meeting 2026 demand.
Slow Ramp-Up
Every new hire carries a $50,000+ hidden cost.
Locked Growth
Manufacturing turnover averages 28%, and a single mis-hire costs 2x the annual salary in rework and lost margin.

Outcomes for Our Partners

Predictable Talent Supply
A reliable pipeline of high-quality talent aligned to special projects.
Faster Time to Productivity
Operators reach productive output sooner, reducing ramp-up delays.
Reduced Hiring & Execution Risk
Objective validation lowers mis-hire rates and quality variability.
Scalable Workforce Capacity
Talent capacity expands in step with manufacturing growth.
Revenue Unlocked
Production timelines are met without talent bottlenecks delaying contracts.
